Tutorials
=========

.. _simplebench-tutorials:

.. index::

.. container:: textblock

    This section provides step-by-step tutorials to help you get started with
    SimpleBench. Whether you're new to benchmarking or looking to deepen your
    understanding of SimpleBench's features, these tutorials will guide you
    through practical examples and use cases.

    Each tutorial is designed to be hands-on, allowing you to follow along
    and implement the concepts in your own projects. From basic benchmarks
    to advanced reporting techniques, you'll find a variety of topics covered
    here.
